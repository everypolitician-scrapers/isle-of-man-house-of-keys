Raymond Karl HARMER MHK (Glenfaba and Peel)

Contact Tel: 01624 685596; Email: Ray.Harmer@gov.im

Personal

Born: 1967

Education: Cambridge University (MA Engineering), MBA Open University

Family: Married to Adele in 1994, 1 son

Career: Marketing product manager (Rational Group), senior business analyst (Zurich, First Names and Nedgroup), networks manager (Royal Life), communications consulting engineer (Ove Arup, London)

Public Service: Peel Commissioner 2009-15 (Chairman 2010-11, May 2013–Sept 2015); Treasurer of Peel Heritage Trust 2014-date; Chairman of Peel Heritage Trust 2016-date

Interests: A member of Peel Golf Club and Western Athletics. I enjoy running and have completed the London and Paris marathons for charity. I play saxophone with the Manx Saxophone Ensemble and have entered the Parish Walk, finishing at Peel several times and completing it twice.

Parliamentary Career

Member: Health and Social Care 2015-16; Infrastructure 2015-16

Committees of Tynwald: Select Committee of Tynwald on the First-Time Buyer Scheme (Petition for Redress) 2015-16

Electoral History

First elected as Member of the House of Keys at a by-election in 2015, re-elected in 2016 for new constituency of Glenfaba and Peel.

Commonwealth Parliamentary Association
